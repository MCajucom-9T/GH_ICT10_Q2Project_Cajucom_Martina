More about us

The University of the Philippines Medical Science (UPMS) is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in medical education, research, and community health service. Guided by the values of integrity, service, and innovation, UPMS prepares future healthcare professionals through rigorous academic training and hands-on clinical exposure. The university is committed to advancing medical knowledge, promoting public health, and cultivating leaders who are equipped to meet the evolving needs of Philippine and global healthcare.
